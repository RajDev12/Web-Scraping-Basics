Pope skips Palm Sunday homily but continues service

Pope Francis surprised attendees at Palm Sunday Mass in St. Peter's Square by forgoing his usual homily reading due to health issues. The 87-year-old pontiff, who has been battling bronchitis and influenza, had an aide read his prepared speech instead. This deviation from tradition raised eyebrows as it is rare for a pope to entirely skip delivering a homily at such a significant event.
